Myths About Hackers: Debunked

Myth: All Hackers are Criminals

Many people associate hackers with cybercrime. However, ethical hackers, or "white hats," work to protect systems and uncover vulnerabilities.

Myth: Hackers Only Work Alone

Contrary to popular belief, many hackers work in teams or collaborate in cybersecurity communities to tackle large-scale threats.

Myth: Hackers Only Use Complex Code

While advanced coding skills are helpful, many successful hacks rely on basic techniques like social engineering or exploiting common vulnerabilities.

Myth: Hackers Don't Get Caught

Many hackers are caught through digital forensics, monitoring, and tracking IP addresses. Law enforcement agencies and ethical hackers work together to bring criminals to justice.
